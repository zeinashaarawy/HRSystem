System Flow

Phase 1 — Job Setup

HR creates job template (REC-003)

HR defines hiring process stages (REC-004)

Phase 2 — Job Posting

Job is published to careers page (REC-023)

Phase 3 — Application

Candidate applies and uploads CV (REC-007)

Candidate gives consent for data processing (REC-028)

Phase 4 — Tagging & Screening

HR tags candidates as referrals (REC-030)

HR moves candidate to stage “Screening”

HR tracks candidate progress through all stages (REC-008)

Phase 5 — Interviews

HR schedules interviews (REC-010)

HR assigns interview panel (REC-021)

Panel adds feedback and scores (REC-011)

Phase 6 — Communication

Candidate receives status notifications (REC-017)

HR sends rejection or progression emails (REC-022)

Phase 7 — Offer

HR manager creates offer (REC-014)

Candidate signs electronic offer (REC-018)

Phase 8 — Handoff to Onboarding

HR triggers onboarding workflow (ONB-004)

New hire receives onboarding tracker and tasks (ONB-004, ONB-005)
